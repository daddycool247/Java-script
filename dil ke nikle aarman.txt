fark tha hum dono ke mohabbat me 
muhje usse hi ishq tha
use mujhe se bhi isqh tha...

vo aaye the mere kafn pe
kon kehta hai daphnane ke baad bhi jalaya nhi jaa sakta...

ankhon ko mehfooz rakhna 
badi der tak raate raat hogi
musafir the hum bhi 
musafir the tum bhi 
phir kisi mod pe mulaqat hogi...

dil me na jane kaise itni jagah ban gayi
uski ek muskurahat meri jeene ki vajah ban gayi...

mohabbat ki shwal lene gaya tha isqh ke baazar me 
logo ne kaha kafn bhi le jao sath me...
